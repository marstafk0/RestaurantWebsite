About Us

Welcome to the Burger Bar! A fantastic place to hangout and watch a Sunday football game! Our burgers are made of quality grass-fed beef, high grade Wisconsin cheese,
and fresh locally produced vegetables. We carry homebrewed beers as well as multiple varieties of IPA's and light beers. Our favorite night is Tuesday night! Delicious
$5 cheese curds every Tuesday along with $5 draft beer!

We started this burger joint out of love for our small little hometown of Cedar Pint Minnesota. Not a bar in the north served a decent burger, it was all wings and
onion rings. Now we serve over 100 people a night, reaching upwards of 200 on a good Friday. We're rated 4.9 across the books, and our staff consists of 35 hard working individuals.
